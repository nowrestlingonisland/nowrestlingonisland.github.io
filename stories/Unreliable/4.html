Unreliable: A tale in several parts

Part 4: Dad was initially hesitant

Dad was initially hesitant to follow Mum's advice to go and see if Sandra needed help - I mean, literal bros before hoes and all that - but eventually he gave in to her pressure and ended up driving to Wellington to give her some emotional support. Turns out he was all too good at giving emotional support; he and Sandra ended up hooking up down there, and after a few days they drove back and appeared at our door holding hands, sheepishly telling Mum that they'd found a new lease on love together, and was it OK if they stayed at ours for a bit while they found a new place together?

Mum wasn't too receptive of their request. In fact, she tore them a new one and sent them packing.
